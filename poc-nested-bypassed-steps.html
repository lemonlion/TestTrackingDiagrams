POC: Great-Grandchild Bypassed Step with Passing Siblings at Every Level

This demonstrates how steps render when a deeply nested sub-step (great-grandchild, 4 levels deep) is bypassed, while every level also has passing sibling steps. Parent steps with any bypassed descendant get the blue tick icon (.passed-bypassed).

Legend: ✓ Passed ↷ Bypassed ✓ Passed (has bypassed descendant)
